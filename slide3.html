Core Components of Our QRNG System

1

555 Timer (Entropy Source)

Generates chaotic analog oscillations approximating quantum unpredictability.

2

Dual D Flip-Flop (74HC74)

Captures instantaneous states of the chaotic signal → converts to crisp digital bits.

3

Arduino Microcontroller

Processes raw bits, performs randomness extraction, outputs final random stream.

4

LED Indicator

Visual monitoring of bit generation & system status.
