How the QRNG Works — Step by Step

01 — Entropy Generation (555 Timer)

Produces a rapidly fluctuating analog signal with unpredictable peaks.

02 — Timed Sampling

The same chip provides a synchronized clock pulse for sampling moments.

03 — State Capture (Flip-Flop)

Captures the exact instantaneous value → outputs a raw binary 0 or 1.

04 — Digital Bit Output

Flip-flop outputs a clean digital random bit.

05 — Randomness Extraction (Arduino)

Arduino filters noise, removes bias, and produces high-quality random numbers.
