Why Quantum Randomness?

Classical randomness is algorithmic → predictable with enough computation.

Quantum randomness arises from fundamental uncertainty → impossible to predict.

Key Points

• Classical generators = deterministic patterns

• Quantum mechanics = true unpredictability

• Measurement outcomes are fundamentally random

• QRNGs are the gold standard for security & cryptography
